Globe SIM Registration Information

Understanding the Globe SIM Registration

About SIM Registration

The Globe SIM Registration Act (Republic Act No. 11934) requires all mobile phone users in the Philippines to register their SIM cards. This initiative aims to promote accountability and prevent fraudulent activities in mobile communications.

Key Information

Required Documents

  • Valid Government-issued ID
  • Active SIM Card
  • Personal Information
  • Proof of Identity

Important Details

  • Registration is mandatory
  • Applies to all mobile users
  • Free of charge
  • One-time process

Benefits of Registration

  • Enhanced security against scams
  • Protection from identity theft
  • Easier recovery of lost numbers
  • Improved accountability in mobile communications
  • Better security for mobile transactions
